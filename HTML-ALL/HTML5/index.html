About Bangalore

Bangalore (Bengaluru) is the capital of Karnataka, India, and a major hub for its IT industry, earning it the nickname "Silicon Valley of India". It is a megacity with over 10 million people, located on the Deccan Plateau in Southern India at an elevation of over 900 meters
